Frequently Asked Questions

What is EasyTripCoin?

EasyTripCoin is a utility cryptocurrency built on the BEP20 protocol, designed specifically for travel-related payments. It enables seamless transactions for hotel bookings, restaurant payments, and other travel expenses with minimal fees and instant settlements.

How do I purchase EasyTripCoin?

You can participate in our token sale using BNB, BUSD, or USDT. Once launched, EasyTripCoin will be available on major decentralized exchanges on the Binance Smart Chain.

Which travel services accept EasyTripCoin?

We are building partnerships with major hotel chains, travel booking platforms, and restaurant groups. Our initial launch partners will be announced before the public token sale, with more partners being added continuously.

What are the benefits for travelers?

EasyTripCoin offers reduced transaction fees, instant settlements, no currency exchange fees, and special discounts with partner establishments. Holders will also get priority access to travel deals and promotions.

Is there a rewards program?

Yes, our Travel Rewards Program will launch in Q4 2025, allowing users to earn additional tokens through regular travel spending and referring new users to the platform.
